About Us

Since 1998, Sushi Corner has been a pioneer in bringing the authentic flavors of Japan to food enthusiasts across the country. With several branches strategically located throughout the nation, we have become a go-to destination for those seeking an exceptional Japanese dining experience. At the heart of our success lies our dedicated team, whose passion and expertise have made us a renowned name in the industry. Our talented chefs, with their years of experience and commitment to perfection, craft each dish with precision and creativity. From the artful presentation of traditional sushi rolls to the nuanced flavors of expertly prepared sashimi, our culinary creations are a testament to their mastery.
